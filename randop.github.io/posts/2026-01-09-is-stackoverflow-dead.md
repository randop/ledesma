### Honest take: **YES**

A moment of silence as we are gathered here to mourn the passing of Stack Overflow (2008 — 2026). The [graph](https://data.stackexchange.com/stackoverflow/query/1926661#graph) shows the question count over the past 17 years telling a brutal story of its **~98% collapse**.
