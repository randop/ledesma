The new AI era is now the world we are living today. People today are using AI as modern tools to search on topics, fact check, generate shorts, do school homework, assist in day-to-day tasks on the workplace. This has created a side-effect syndrome referred today as **"AI Psychosis"**. 

### What is AI Psychosis?
It is a psychological induced pressure on all parties involved with using, accusing, teaching and mandating Artificial Intelligence (AI). All sorts of mental pressures have always existed and continues on our lifetime. And now, this includes AI psychosis, that is affecting our daily lives.

### Next...
There is not yet a massive success story that attributes AI as the sole reason. No student or teacher have come out to proclaim that AI created this new educational paradigm that shocked the world. No company or CEO or sales team have gone to public announcing that by them using AI have resulted in record-breaking profits by product created by AI. The next thing that can happen is people will love and hate AI as part of living in this new reality. The AI psychosis will not go away and will everyday cause issues just like how the Internet, social media, and other things on this world that gives pressure on us.
